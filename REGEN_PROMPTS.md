# Regen Your Real Photos → Polished AI Style

Goal: take each of your real bakes and produce a **polished, magazine-quality** version that still shows YOUR cake — same shape, same flavors, same garnish — just cleaner background, softer light, professional styling.

## Best FREE tool for this

**Google Gemini (gemini.google.com)** — it lets you upload your photo AND describe a transformation. This is real image-to-image. Free with any Google account.

Backups:
- **ChatGPT free** — upload photo + ask for polished version (works, more limited per day)
- **Microsoft Designer / Bing** — paste the prompt only (won't reference your photo, but will generate a fresh version that matches the description)
- **Krea AI / Leonardo AI** — free tiers with proper image-to-image sliders

## Workflow per photo

1. Open Gemini → click 📎 → upload one of your photos
2. Paste the photo's specific prompt below
3. Generate → download → save into `images/` with the **non-`_photo` filename** (e.g., `rasmalai-tres-leches.jpeg`)
4. The catalog will auto-use the polished version since it's already wired to those filenames.

## Master style (used in every prompt)

> *Re-render this dessert as polished professional food photography: soft natural window light, clean cream-white linen or marble background, shallow depth of field, gentle warm color grading, magazine quality, ultra-detailed, 4K. Keep the dessert exactly as shown — same shape, same toppings, same colors. Only change the background, lighting, and styling to look more elevated.*
